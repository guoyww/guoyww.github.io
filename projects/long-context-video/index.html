Interactive Multi-shot Extension

Our model enables interactive multi-shot development. This facilitates an iterative production workflow where directors can progressively shape content shot-by-shot based on previously generated footage, eliminating the need for comprehensive upfront prompting and allowing for creative decision-making with immediate visual feedback.

Interactive Single-shot Extension

our model enables interactive shot extension without shotcut. This approach can extend a single shot to minute-long durations, auto-regressively generating 10-second segments while maintaining visual consistency.

Composable Generation

Remarkably, despite no explicit training for this capability, our model enables composable generation by accepting separate identity and environment images to synthesize coherent videos integrating these distinct elements.
